ESOP Based Compensation:

Many startup employees receive part of their compensation in the form of stock options or equity grants.These are valuable assets that can potentially yield significant returns if the startup succeeds and goes public or gets acquired.

Unlocking ESOPs:

Plutus allows employees with ESOPS to unlock the value of their stock options by connecting them with a group of investors on the platform.

Investorâ€™s Role & Gain:

Investors on Plutus provide the necessary funds to help employees exercise their stock options. In return, these investors receive a share of the future gains if the startup's value increases plus they also gain a way to participate in the startups at an early stage, which was previously unattainable. This allows employees to exercise their ESOPs without the need to pay the upfront costs themselves.

Risk-Sharing:

Plutus essentially creates a risk-sharing model. If the startup's value grows, both the employees and the investors benefit. If the startup's value doesn't increase, the investors may not see a return on their investment, but the employees are not required to repay the funds they received.

Win-Win Solution:

Plutus aims to be a win-win solution for both startup employees and investors. Employees can access their equity without financial burden, while investors can potentially profit from their investments in promising startups.
